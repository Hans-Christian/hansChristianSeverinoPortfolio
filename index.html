About Me

So, where do I begin? For starters, I’m a ‘90s kid at heart! I also love running and weightlifting, spending time with family, doing outdoor activities, Nintendo, and competitively playing board games that jeopardize relationships (e.g., Settlers of Catan).


For school, I graduated from Seneca College for Business Marketing. However, I didn’t feel that fiery passion to make a career out of it, so I decided to look elsewhere. In early 2019, I had dinner with a good friend who talked about his experience as a Backend Developer. It was that meeting that convinced me to look into Juno College for Front-End Development. I eventually joined their Intro to Web Dev course later that year and fell in love with coding. As of December 2020, I can proudly say that I’m a graduate of the Cohort 30, Web Development Bootcamp!
